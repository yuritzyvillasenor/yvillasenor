About me.

I have a passion for education and for my community. I was raised in southeast Fresno and still live in the neighborhood where I grew up. I have 12 years of experience in education, six years as an administrator. It is because of my experiences as I worked with children, parents, teachers and staff that I am an advocate of education. more ›





What I Believe.
I believe that our children deserve a quality education. Our communities deserve well-prepared citizens and that begins in the classrooms.



The mission & plan.
As a trustee member of the Fresno Unified Board, it is my goal to maximize the amount of resources used to serve our students. My objective is to adequately educate our children to achieve academically, socially, emotionally and physically at the optimal level. All of our students need to succeed now to ensure success in the future.
